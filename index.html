Our Story

Welcome to Papaya Juice, your cozy corner in Mohammedia, Morocco. Born from a passion for fresh, healthy living, we bring you the finest selection of handcrafted juices, smoothies, and café delights.

We source the freshest local fruits and ingredients to create vibrant, nutritious drinks that capture the essence of Moroccan hospitality. Whether you're looking for a morning energy boost or a refreshing afternoon treat, Papaya Juice is your destination.

Come experience the warmth of Mohammedia with a glass of sunshine!
